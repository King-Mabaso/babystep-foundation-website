About Us

BabyStep Foundation [LIMP] is a community-based organization dedicated to improving the lives of people in Malawi.

Mission

Alleviating lives of people from poverty through action-based initiatives.

Vision

To improve the well-being of lives of people at individual and family level by 2035.

We believe that small, consistent steps today create big changes tomorrow.
